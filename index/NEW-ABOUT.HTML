About KLEENORA

Pakistan's leading provider of premium cleaning solutions, trusted by over 50,000 customers nationwide.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2020, CleanIt Store began with a simple mission: to provide Pakistani households and businesses with access to world-class cleaning products that deliver exceptional results while being safe for families and the environment. What started as a small family business has grown into Pakistan's most trusted cleaning brand, serving customers from Karachi to Lahore, Islamabad to Peshawar, and everywhere in between. Today, we're proud to offer over 100 premium cleaning products across household, automotive, and industrial categories, all backed by our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
